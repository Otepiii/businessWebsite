General Contractor / Project Manager

Submit a bid to the owner

projectpartner allows General contractors and Project Managers to expedite the bidding process which is an incredibly integral part of a construction project. This process allows general contractors to submit a bid to the owner at a competitive rate.

To Start, follow these steps


  • Tell the system - The cost and division

    •     ie $1500 for cleaning, $1000 for painting - items are broken out by MasterFormat



  • Tell Subtrades when to have their BIDS in to you (NOTE: This is not the official closing Date & Time - this is your deadline so you have time to review)

NOTE: ENTER BIDDING ENGINE WALKTHROUGH HERE


  • Select if you want bids to include "Exclusions Omissions" or all works per division

    •     ie all electrical works indrawings and specs


  • Select if you want subtrades to quote with "Alternative Works" to the drawings or specs

    •     ie Brand name Tyveck is used in bid documents, but a alternative product that meets or exceeds spec is acceptable
